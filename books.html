How to Read Two Books a Week

  1. Try to stick to books from 200-400 pages
  2. Choose the genre that you mostly watch via tv or movies
  3. Try to read at least one non ficiton book a week to help with learning of various topics
  4. Find out how many the pages the book is without the table of contents and introduction
  5. Take the number of pages in the book divided by 7
  6. Read that amount of pages everyday from each book
  7. Another method I use sometimes is to take one book divided by 3 and antoher by 4.
