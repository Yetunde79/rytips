How to make detergent

Ingredients needed

  • 3.5 cups of borax
  • Half the package of washing soda *NOT baking soda*
  • A bar of quality soap. If you want to use cheaper soap an example is: Ivory use 3 soap bars
  • Full package 1 pound of oxy clean
  • Highly recommended you pour in scent crystals to get that great smell

Steps

  1. You need a cheese grater,bucket with a top, gloves or a spoon to stir
  2. Use the cheese grater to make the soap as thin as possible
  3. Measure out ingredients
  4. Place everything in a container and mix
  5. You are done and have just saved yourself at least 50 dollars a year
  6. *Also this combination is strong so you really only need a table spoon or two max per load
